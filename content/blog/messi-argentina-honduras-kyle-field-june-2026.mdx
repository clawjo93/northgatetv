# Messi at Kyle Field? Argentina vs. Honduras Coming to College Station June 6

![Kyle Field to host soccer exhibition between Argentina and Honduras — KBTX Breaking News](/blog/argentina-honduras-kyle-field.jpg)

The biggest soccer event in Aggieland history is about to go down — and it's happening right here in College Station.

Texas A&M's **Kyle Field** will host an international soccer friendly between **Argentina and Honduras on Saturday, June 6, 2026** — just days before the FIFA World Cup kicks off. Texas A&M, the City of College Station, United Events, and Florida Citrus Sports confirmed the matchup Thursday morning.

---

## Why This Match Is a Massive Deal

This isn't just any friendly. This is a **World Cup warm-up** for the reigning world champions — and it's happening in your backyard.

Argentina won it all in Qatar in 2022 and are one of the top 5 favorites to win again this summer. Their roster is loaded with some of the biggest names in world football:

- **Lionel Messi** — 8x Ballon d'Or, widely considered the greatest of all time. At 38 years old, this could be his final World Cup appearance ever
- **Julián Álvarez** — Atlético Madrid striker and World Cup 2022 hero
- **Alexis Mac Allister** — Liverpool midfielder
- **Cristian Romero** — Tottenham center back

Honduras did not qualify for the 2026 World Cup, making this a critical final tune-up for La Albiceleste before they enter Group J play on June 16 in Kansas City against Algeria.

---

## Kyle Field Goes International — Again

This will be only the **second time in Kyle Field's 122-year history** that it has hosted an international soccer match. In 2024, 85,249 fans packed the stadium to watch Brazil edge Mexico 3-2 in a legendary 96th-minute finish by Endrick.

If Messi is on the pitch — and all signs point to yes — Kyle Field could see a capacity crowd of **102,773 fans**. That would make it one of the largest attended soccer matches on U.S. soil and put College Station on the global soccer map.
