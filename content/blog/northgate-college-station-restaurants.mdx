Northgate is not just about the bars. The district has a real food scene — from iconic spots that have been feeding Aggies since 1974 to outdoor grills, late-night tacos, and everything in between. Here is where to eat on and around the strip in 2026.

---

## The Classics

### Dixie Chicken ⭐

**307 University Drive, College Station TX 77840 · (979) 846-2322**

**Hours:** Sun–Thu 11am–Midnight · Fri–Sat 11am–2am · **Price: $**

The oldest bar on Northgate is also one of the most important eating experiences on the strip — not because of the food alone, but because of what surrounds it. Dixie Chicken has been serving cheap, classic burgers and fried food since 1974 inside a room covered in rattlesnake floorboards, thousands of longneck bottles, and decades of Aggie memorabilia.

Come for the **Ring Dunk** tradition, stay for the burgers and the people-watching. This is Aggie culture in a building.

**Order:** Classic burger, cold longneck, and take everything in.

---

### The Corner Bar & Rooftop Grill

**401 University Drive, College Station TX 77840 · (979) 268-1406**

**Hours:** Wed–Fri 5pm–2am · Sat 11am–2am · Closed Sun–Mon · **Price: $$**

Three floors, three bars, and food served throughout — but the rooftop is where you want to eat. Views of campus and the strip, cold beer, solid pub grub. Family-owned since 2004 and one of the most welcoming spots in the district for visiting fans and first-timers. Excellent pre-game meal spot if you want to settle in and stay all afternoon.

**Order:** Whatever is on tap plus something from the grill before the game.

---

### The Backyard on Northgate

**303 University Drive East, College Station TX 77840 · (979) 846-8806**

**Hours:** Mon–Sat 10am–2am · Sunday Closed · **Price: $$**

The largest outdoor space on the entire strip — a sprawling beer garden with a fire pit, cornhole, games, and the biggest screen in the district. Food is served throughout and the outdoor grill runs during peak hours. The best spot on Northgate for a large group that wants to eat, drink, and actually have room to move around.

**Best for:** Groups, outdoor dining, game watching, casual hangouts.

## Late Night Eats

When the bars wind down, these are the spots keeping people fed.

### Torchy's Tacos

**Near Northgate District · College Station TX · Price: $**

A Texas institution that needs no introduction. Torchy's serves loaded, irreverent tacos with bold flavors and generous portions. Open late on weekends — one of the best post-bar fuel stops in the area.

**Order:** Trailer Park, Dirty Sanchez. You will understand.

---

### Freebird's World Burrito

**Near Northgate District · College Station TX · Price: $**

The college student's best friend. Massive burritos built exactly how you want them — fresher ingredients than your average chain and portions that actually do the job. Open late on weekends. Less glamorous than a sit-down meal and perfectly right for what it is.

**Order:** Whatever burrito your body is asking for at midnight.

---

### Wings 'N More

**College Station TX · Near Northgate · Price: $–$$**

A Northgate staple that has been delivering on the basics for years. Great wings, solid sauces, cold beer, plenty of TVs. The kind of place locals keep coming back to because it never lets you down. Works just as well for a mid-week dinner as a pre-game session.

**Order:** Wings sampler with multiple sauces.

---

## Game Day Food Tips

Eat before the rush. Restaurants near Northgate are packed 2–3 hours before kickoff and wait times spike fast. Get your meal in early or go straight to the late-night quick-service spots post-game — Torchy's and Freebird's are built for high volume and move fast even when the district is at capacity.

Several bars also serve food — check if your venue of choice has a menu and eat there to avoid the restaurant queue entirely.
