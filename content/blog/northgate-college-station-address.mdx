If you are heading to Northgate for the first time — or trying to tell out-of-town friends where to meet — this is everything you need to know about finding the district, getting there, and navigating it once you arrive.

---

## Where Is Northgate?

Northgate sits on the **north side of Texas A&M University** in College Station, Texas. The district runs along:

- **College Avenue** — the main spine of the strip
- **University Drive** — the southern boundary
- **Nagle Street** and **Stasney Street** — the main cross streets

The Texas A&M north gate opens directly onto the southern end of the Northgate strip — which is how the district got its name.

**GPS Address:** 307 University Drive, College Station TX 77840 *(Dixie Chicken — dead center of the strip)*

---

## Distance From Campus

For Texas A&M students Northgate is essentially zero distance. The north gate of campus opens directly onto the strip.

- From **Kyle Field**: 5–8 minute walk north
- From **Memorial Student Center**: 10–12 minute walk
- From **Northside dorms**: under 10 minutes on foot

---

## Directions From Major Cities

### From Houston
Take **Highway 6 North** into College Station. Northgate is on the north side of the Texas A&M campus.
**Drive time:** ~1.5–2 hours depending on traffic.

### From Austin
Take **Highway 21 East** or **Highway 79** through Bryan-College Station.
**Drive time:** ~1.5–2 hours.

### From Dallas
Take **Highway 6 South** through Hearne into College Station.
**Drive time:** ~2.5–3 hours.

### From Bryan
Head south on **Texas Avenue / Highway 6**. Take University Drive East toward campus.
**Drive time:** 10–15 minutes.

---

## Getting Around Once You Are There

Northgate is fully walkable. Once you park or get dropped off, everything is within easy walking distance. The main strip along College Avenue runs about 10–15 minutes end to end on foot.

**College Avenue is your home base.** Most bars and restaurants are directly on it or one block off it. If you get turned around, find College Avenue and you are back on track.

---

## Rideshare

Uber and Lyft operate heavily in College Station and are the most reliable way to get to and from Northgate — especially on game days when parking is impossible and you should not be driving anyway.

**Pro tip:** Set your pickup location one block off the main strip before last call. The rideshare queue directly on College Avenue at 2am is chaotic. Walk half a block, get picked up in seconds.

---

## Public Transit

**Texas A&M Aggie Spirit Shuttle** has stops near Northgate. Check current routes at transport.tamu.edu.

**Bryan-College Station B-CS Transit** also serves the area. Check btxbus.com for current schedules.
