Northgate shifted in 2025. A few spots that had been around forever finally closed. New names moved in. The district is rebuilding with fresh energy and some of the biggest bar openings in years. This is the complete, confirmed list of every bar currently operating in the Northgate District as of 2026 — addresses included, no outdated information.

## New in 2026

### Harry's Northgate

**217 University Drive at Boyett Street, College Station TX 77840**

The most anticipated opening Northgate has seen in years. Harry's is 11,000 square feet sitting at the former Shiner Park location at 217 University Drive. Expanded dance floor, a live music stage, DJ nights, and two-stepping brought back to Aggieland. This is immediately one of the biggest venues on the strip and a natural anchor for the district going forward.

**Vibe:** Country Bar / Dance Hall | **Status:** New Spring 2026 | **Instagram:** @harrysbcs

---

### Rush Northgate

**Former Foundation Lounge Space, Northgate District**

Built on the old Foundies space, Rush Northgate brings a Houston-inspired dual concept to the district. Cocktail bar in the evening, full nightclub from 11pm to close. Rotating DJs every Saturday to keep the atmosphere fresh, neon interiors, and a vision to push Northgate's club scene to the level of what's normal in Houston. One of the most ambitious new concepts in College Station nightlife.

**Vibe:** Cocktail Bar + Nightclub | **Status:** Opened 2025 | **Instagram:** @rushnorthgate

## Classics Still Running Strong

### Dixie Chicken

**307 University Drive, College Station TX 77840 — Est. 1974**

The oldest bar on Northgate and one of the most iconic college bars in Texas. Rattlesnake floorboards, longneck bottles, domino tables, Aggie memorabilia covering every wall. It's been here since 1974 and they're stepping into 2026 stronger and more committed to protecting what makes Historic Northgate special than ever. If you do one bar on Northgate, it's this one.

**Vibe:** Iconic Dive Bar | **Instagram:** @thedixiechicken

---

### Good Bull Icehouse

**205 University Drive, College Station TX 77840**

College Station's only bar with a massive outdoor screen in the back and a full nightclub upstairs. Sprawling game day patio, specialty drinks including Capri Funs and Mega Mules, bottle service and section rentals, and live music bookings. Open Tuesday through Saturday from 3pm to 2am. One of the most complete venues on the strip for any occasion.

**Vibe:** Sports Bar / Icehouse + Nightclub Upstairs | **Instagram:** @goodbullicehouse

---

### 12 Rooftop Bar and Lounge

**209 University Drive, College Station TX 77840**

Two experiences stacked on top of each other. Open-air rooftop bar upstairs with city views and a relaxed crowd, full dance club downstairs when the night picks up. One of the most versatile spots on the strip — you can chill or turn up without leaving the building.

**Vibe:** Rooftop Bar + Dance Club | **Instagram:** @12rooftopbar

### The Corner Bar and Grill

**401-405 University Drive, College Station TX 77840 — Est. 2004**

One of the most adaptable businesses in the district's history. The rooftop patio is one of the best views on Northgate, and the Corner Bar has stayed relevant by evolving with the market — adding Polite Coffee for the lunch crowd and adjusting hours to serve more than just the nightlife window. Game day, weeknight, or full Saturday, it works for all of it.

**Vibe:** Rooftop + Full Restaurant | **Website:** thecornerbarcs.com

**Northgate Proper** — No longer operating as of 2026.

## Tips for a Northgate Night in 2026

**Thursday is the new Friday.** Aggies treat Thursday like the start of the weekend. The district fills up fast and most bars are running full Thursday-night specials.

**Don't drive in.** Parking on University Drive is a battle on weekends. Rideshare or use the Northgate Parking Garage on College Main and walk the strip.

**Bar hop on foot.** Every bar on this list is within walking distance of the others. That's how Northgate is meant to be experienced — start at one end and work your way through.

**Construction is almost done.** The ongoing Northgate construction projects are expected to wrap in the next 18 months. More pedestrian traffic and more business energy is coming.
